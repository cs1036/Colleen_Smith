# Daisy

DAISIES
Basic info
Daisies are classic, sun-loving plants belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by their ring of petals surrounding a central disk. Famous for following the sun and closing at night.

They are highly popular as:
Bedding plants
Borders plants
Cut flowers

Popular Varieties & The "Cape Daisy"
Beautiful daisies
Cape Daisies (Osteospermum): Indigenous to Southern Africa, these thrive in full sun and are heat-tolerant. They come in vibrant purples, whites, and yellows.
Marguerites (Argyranthemum): Traditional, shrub-like daisies with delicate foliage, ideal for pots or smaller gardens.
English Daisies (Bellis perennis): Low-growing and compact, perfect for lawns and ground cover.
